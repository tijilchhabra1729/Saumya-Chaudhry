Evolve

Year

2020

Size

2732 x 2048 pixels

Medium

Digital Sketch

Description

As I sit within the welcoming rays of the winter sun next to my grandmother, she tells me the story of her family attempting to find shelter during the anti-Sikh (a religious sect in India) riots in India, one that caused the death of more than 2,000 Sikhs.


“We did not feel the sun for days, buried underground.”, she tells me.


My artwork imagines the solace she must’ve felt, when finally free of the dangers that lie outside. The monochromatic checks and crosses symbolize the remnants of riots, but the human figure stands in solace still. Letting the welcoming rays reach her soul.
