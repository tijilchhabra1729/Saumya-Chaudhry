Intihaa (Zine)

Year

2020

Size

1080 x 1080 pixels

Medium

Digital Illustrations

Description

If the world is unsympathetic… why must I listen to my heart at all? The idea of coming out as a lesbian always haunted me. Additionally, for many in the LGBTQIA+ community, coming out is never easy. It presents trials that force them to hide behind the frame, always escaping their realities.


Especially in a country like India, lesbians are often forced to court men… all in an attempt to trap them into heteronormativity. The scribble-esque background on each page of the zine delineates the chaos in my mind.


My face, remains obstructed -- a symbol of my fear. Thus, employing colors like Stone and Ebony, I used the digital medium to illustrate my process of coming out. One that constantly puts me at the edge of the screen, leaving me to hide, to be trapped in my own extremities.
