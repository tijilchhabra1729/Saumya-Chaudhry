Mona Lisa

Year

2021

Size

2732 x 2048 pixels

Medium

Sketch and digital illustration

Description

What art counts as beautiful? What art is pretty? Who decides what makes me an artist?


Though technical ability beautifully supplements the work of any artist, what does it truly mean to be creative?


The artwork here represents an artist's anguish to display technical skill. The sketching of the vase is left incompleted, spoiled by an impulsive stroke of frustration. It seems as if the artist says, “I quit.”.


So what is ideal art, if not rebellion?
