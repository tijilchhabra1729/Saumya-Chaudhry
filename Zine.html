Safe (Zine)

Year

2022

Size

2732 x 2048 pixels

Medium

Digital Illustrations

Description

My mother would tell me tales, of princesses being whisked away to foreign lands, by their Prince Charming -- an image that would bring me discomfort. My grand story of love was with a woman, and I longed to see that story on a screen, even within the pages of a book.


Representation of people who belong to the LGBTQIA+ community within films, books, and popular media, is often non-existent or improper. This zine imitates a fairytale that people from the community often yearn to look at.


The colors, inspired by the shades of the lesbian pride flag, enrich the subtle human figures on each page. These human figures are monochromatic, forming an impression that love inevitably binds the souls of two people.


Two women in love, intertwined, inseparable -- an image that brings me safety.
