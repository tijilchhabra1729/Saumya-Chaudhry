Aristotle

Year

Size

Medium

Description

The words of Aristotleâ€™s Metaphysics are a direct comparison of human existence. My perception of his observations suggests people are more than their predispositions -- that they are complicated versions of the people they meet and the instances they experience.


What does it mean to be two people in love with each other? Is it merely what the sentence says? The artwork, a portrait of two women, is a conduit to their histories -- with varying identities, struggles, and challenges.


Though separate in their respective worlds, these figures stylistically symbolize my retelling of any modern love story: Truly a whole greater than the sum of its parts.
