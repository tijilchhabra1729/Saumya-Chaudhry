The Vessel

Year

2021

Size

16 x 16 in

Medium

Gouache and acrylic painting

Description

How do we open the doors to vessels shut tight? Emotions waiting to spill out onto the ground, but held back forcefully. One such emotion is the feeling in your throat right before tears spill out onto your face, the feeling of wrenching pain that no amount of water can subside.


So we let the tears fall, we let the vessel open, and we let the wounds heal. The monochromatic hands symbolize the universality of a feeling that may feel quite foreign.
