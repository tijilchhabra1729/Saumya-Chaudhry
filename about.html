About

Who am I?

My biggest nightmare is having to tell my story, when it has merely begun. But, with an untiring passion for the creative arts, mental health and activism for social equity, I am currently a 16 year old aspiring artist/psychologist who aims to create art beyond the confines of a canvas. Art, of all realms and kinds has always been my forte. From a sixth grader who thought their poetry could change the world to a twelfth grader who paints like they breathe, I bring with me that very spirit to ever creative project I undertake.

While also increasing my technical expertise in art, as a self-taught artist, I have always looked to other artists for inspiration, growth and diversification in my own work.

The world to me is beautiful, filled with colours, love and more. through my brush strokes, or measly words I hope to connect this world and bind it with the common spirit of art.
