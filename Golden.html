Golden

Year

2021

Size

2732 x 2048 pixels

Medium

Sketch and digital illustration

Description

Ladki paraya dhan hoti hai. (Women are strangers to an Indian family, only born to be married off.)


This statement often acts as a passing excuse in India. A statement that justifies the treatment an Indian woman often goes through. Married into an abusive household, this artwork tells the story of Seema didi (a seamstress near my residence).


The artwork and its interplay of black and white signify the dichotomy of Seemaâ€™s marriage: the dangers she faced along with her inability to leave the abusive household. The granulated strokes are a testament to the scars that often appeared on her arms.


The treatment of women truly mirrors an object -- uncared for and overlooked. Like golden jewels within a locked cupboard.
